About Me

Hi, I’m Gibz — a VR creator and web developer. I design immersive VRChat worlds and craft digital assets that blend creativity with code.

My journey started with Unity and Blender, building VRChat maps and avatars. Now I’m expanding into web development, combining technical workflows with artistic vision.

My mission: to create interactive spaces that feel alive, social-friendly, and visually striking.
